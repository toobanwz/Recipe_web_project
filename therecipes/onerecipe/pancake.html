Apple Pancake

minutesPrep Time : 10 minutes |  Cook Time : 15 minutes minutes | Total Time : 25 minutes

The perfect healthy morning breakfast recipe is prepared with the traditional pancake batter and apple toppings.

Servings: 10  |  Calories : 125  |  Course: Breakfast  |  Cuisine: International  |  Diet: Vegan


Ingredients


1 apple

2 cup maida

2 tbsp sugar

¼ tsp salt

1 tsp baking powder

¼ tsp baking soda

2 tbsp melted butter

1 glass milk

pinch cinnamon powder

1 tsp vanilla extract

2 tsp vinegar


Instructions


  1.   firstly, in a large bowl take 2 cup maida, 2 tbsp sugar, ¼ tsp salt, 1 tsp baking powder and ¼ tsp baking soda.

  2.   mix well making sure everything is well combined.

  3.   add 2 tbsp melted butter and 1 glass milk.

  4.   whisk and mix well making sure everything is well combined.

  5.   add more milk if required to adjust the consistency of the batter.

  6.   now grate 1 apple, pinch cinnamon powder, 1 tsp vanilla extract and 2 tsp vinegar.

  7.   mix well making sure everything is well combined.

  8.   grease the non-stick frying pan with butter and pour ¼ cup pancake batter over the medium-hot pan.

  9.   cook for 1 minute or until the bubbles appear on the surface.

  10.   now flip it over and continue to cook until it is cooked through.

  11.   finally, transfer Apple Pancake Recipe to a plate and serve with maple syrup, honey, and extra butter.

Serve with butter and honey on top.
