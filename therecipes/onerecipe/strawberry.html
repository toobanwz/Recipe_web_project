Strawberry Milkshake

Prep Time : 10 minutes |  Total Time : 10 minutes

A sweet and refreshing strawberry milkshake is the perfect drink for summer. They can be enjoyed as a healthy breakfast beverage, for an energizing snack or even as a light(er) dessert.

Servings: 2  |  Calories : 261  |  Course: Brevages/Breakfast  |  Cuisine: World  |  Diet: Gluten-Free, Vegetarian


Ingredients


250 grams strawberries or 22 to 25 small to medium strawberries

2 cups milk cold or chilled, you can also add 2.5 cups milk

3 to 4 tablespoons raw sugar or any sweetener of choice â€“ add as required, optional

2 to 3 scoops strawberry ice cream or vanilla ice cream, optional


Instructions


  1.   First rinse the strawberries very well in water using a strainer or colander. Drain all of the water.

  2.   Slice off the green leafy part from all the strawberries. Then chop the strawberries.

  3.   Add the chopped strawberries to a blender together with 2 cups of cold or chilled milk.

  4.   Optionally add the raw sugar or as needed. If the strawberries are very sweet, then you don't need to add any sugar or sweetener of choice.

  5.   To make a thicker and creamier shake, add 2 to 3 scoops of vanilla or strawberry ice cream in the blender.

  6.   Blend until smooth.

  7.   Pour in tall glasses and serve strawberry milkshake immediately. You can also add a scoop of vanilla ice cream or strawberry ice cream and have a strawberry ice cream shake.
