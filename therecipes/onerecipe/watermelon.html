Watermelon Agua Fresca

minutesPrep Time : 15 minutes |  Total Time : 15 minutes

The Mexican Agua Fresca is simply a refreshing beverage/drink which does not contain any alcohol. Perfect to beat the heat during summers.

Servings: 5  |  Calories : 78  |  Course: Breverages  |  Cuisine: Mexican  |  Diet: Gluten-Free, Vegetarian, low fat


Ingredients


4 to 5 cups chopped watermelon (deseeded)

1 to 2 cups water or add as required

½ inch chopped ginger

1 tablespoon lemon juice or as required

¼ cup sugar or add as required, optional

5 to 7 ice cubes (optional)

for garnish:

few mint leaves or mint sprig


Instructions


  1.   Chop half of a large watermelon. If you prefer you can use the entire watermelon too. Remove the seeds while chopping watermelon.
  2.   Blend all the chopped watermelon cubes, ginger, lemon juice, sugar and water in a blender.

  3.   Keep a large bowl or pan below a medium mesh strainer.

  4.   Pour the juice on the strainer.

  5.   Press the pulp on the strainer with the back of a spoon so that the extra juice also filters through.

  6.   If you want you can skip this step of straining the juice.

  7.   Discard the pulp.

  8.   Pour the agua fresca in glasses and add a few ice cubes if you like.

  9.   Serve watermelon agua fresca straight away garnished with lemon slices or mint sprigs. You can also enjoy the drink as is without the ice cubes and garnishes.
